About Our Restaurant
Established in 2005, Capri's Restaurant has been serving burgers and sandwiches to Chicago's residents for over a decade. The recipe has been passed down for generations all the way from the island of Capri, constantly being moulded and improved all the way from our chef's hands to your mouths. A family restaurant, come in to sit, relax, and enjoy your dining experience.
